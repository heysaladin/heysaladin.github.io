ABOUT ME

A few things about me.

Hello, my name is Muhammad Sholahuddin (Saladin) who lives on the island of Bali, Indonesia. I am a designer of digital products.

Researching user experience, understanding the problem, and compiling an idea solution for a problem are things I really like.

In the process of compiling a solution there needs to be a tangible form of the solution that is by prototyping. I like to prototype and experiment with user interactions to help find solutions to problems encountered during the research process.

To ensure the prototype that I and the team propose remains easy to use and remains in good performance, frontend development also needs to be considered. And I also know little about the frontend development process.

How art influenced me to become a designer

I like to draw from a young age, when I was in middle school I began to know art, music and theater. All of that made me have a sense of art in me and this really helped me in designing a balance between the value of usability and aesthetics.
